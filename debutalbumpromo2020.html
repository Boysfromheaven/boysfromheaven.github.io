The Great Discovery is the upcoming debut album by Danish rock band Boys From Heaven, released through Target Records. The album will be released worldwide on 23 October 2020.
Inspired by AOR legends like Journey and Toto, Boys From Heaven embody the iconic, grandiose sound with a twist. The great eighties captured in the modern studio.
The band's 2017 EP, which was accompanied by a sold out release concert, was described as "probably the strongest and most complete Danish debut release this year" by Bands of Tomorrow. Since then group's online fan base has more than quadrupled, and they have played established Danish venues and festivals such as Posten Odense, the DR Concert House, Parkrock and Kloften Festival. In 2020 the band signed with Danish independent label Target Records (Mike Tramp, Soren Andersen, Tue West). The Great Discovery has already received praise from critics. The band's single "Convictions" was debut at GAFFA, Denmark's leading music magazine.
