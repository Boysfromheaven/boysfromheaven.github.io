About

Boys from Heaven is a melodic rock-band consisting of 7 friends from Copenhagen. Polyphonic choir, cheesy hooks and Huey-Lewis-Saxophone are some of the things that we identfy with. Deeply inspired by the 80's rock- and pop-scene, we hope to find a place in your heart and at a venue near you!
